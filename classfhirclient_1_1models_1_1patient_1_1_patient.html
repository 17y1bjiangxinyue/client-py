Detailed Description

Information about a person or animal receiving health care services.

Scope and Usage This Resource covers data about persons and animals involved in a wide range of health-related activities, including:

  • Curative activities
  • Psychiatric care
  • Social services
  • Pregnancy care
  • Nursing and assisted living
  • Dietary services
  • Tracking of personal health and exercise data The data in the Resource covers the "who" information about the patient: its attributes are focused on the demographic information necessary to support the administrative, financial and logistic procedures. A Patient record is generally created and maintained by each organization providing care for a patient. A person or animal receiving care at multiple organizations may therefore have its information present in multiple Patient Resources.
